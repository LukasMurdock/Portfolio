  • Egyptian Rat Screw (Card Game)
    Tools Used:

    Rules:
    • Players deal cards until an attack card is played or the table is slappable
    • When an attack card is played, next player gets following # of chances:
    • Attack Cards: Jack - 1, Queen - 2, King - 3, Ace - 4
    • If the defender cannot play an attack card in the given chances, he/she
    - loses the round and the attacker takes all cards on the table.
    • While playing, players can also slap if the following condition is satisfied:
    • Slap Conditions: Double, Sandwich, and Marraige
    - Double: 2 cards of the same number in a row
    - Sandwich: 2 cards of the same number with a card in between
    - Marraige: King and Queen on top of each other

    Features:
    • Implemented ERS in Python with 100% pure logic.
    • Considered ALL possible cases of dealing (100+ cases).
    • Gave PC players different skill levels and made them compete against
    - each other when the table is slappable.
    • Considered the case that the user slaps the table when not supposed to
    - and created a trash deck.
